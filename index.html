Our Food Menu

  • Dinner

    At our hostel, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse and delightful dining experience, with dinner menus that change daily to keep your taste buds excited. Each evening, our talented chefs craft a variety of dishes that showcase the freshest local ingredients and culinary creativity. From hearty comfort food to exotic international flavors, there's always something new to savor. Whether you're in the mood for a classic pasta dish, a vibrant stir-fry, or a gourmet vegetarian option, our dinners are designed to cater to all dietary preferences and appetites. As you gather with fellow travelers in our welcoming dining area, you'll enjoy not only delicious food but also the chance to share stories and make new friends, turning each meal into a memorable part of your stay.

  • Lunch

    At our hostel, lunch is a delightful experience that varies daily to keep your taste buds excited and satisfied. Each day, our talented chefs craft a unique menu that showcases a diverse range of cuisines, ensuring there's always something new to look forward to. Whether it's a hearty Italian pasta, a spicy Thai curry, a comforting bowl of ramen, or a fresh and vibrant Mediterranean salad, our lunch offerings are designed to cater to a variety of preferences and dietary needs. We emphasize fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create wholesome and delicious meals that not only nourish but also celebrate the rich tapestry of global flavors. Our ever-changing lunch menu guarantees that you'll never have the same meal twice, making each midday meal at our hostel a culinary adventure.

  • Breakfast

    At our hostel, we take pride in offering a delightful breakfast experience that changes daily to keep things exciting and cater to diverse tastes. Each morning, guests are greeted with a fresh and varied selection of delicious Nepali meals to kickstart their day. From hearty classics like **Aloo Paratha** (stuffed potato flatbread) served with curd and pickles, to lighter fare such as **Chiura** (beaten rice) accompanied by spicy potato curry and fresh fruits, there's something for everyone. Our rotating menu ensures that no two breakfasts are the same, featuring traditional dishes like **Sel Roti** (a rice flour doughnut), **Gundruk** (fermented leafy greens), and **Juju Dhau** (a creamy yogurt from Bhaktapur). Whether you're craving a savory start or a sweet treat, our breakfast spread is designed to provide the perfect balance of nutrition and indulgence, setting you up for a day of exploration and adventure in Nepal.
